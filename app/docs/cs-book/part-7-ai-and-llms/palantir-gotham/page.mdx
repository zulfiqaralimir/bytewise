# Palantir Gotham

**Palantir Gotham** is a data-analysis and intelligence platform developed by **Palantir Technologies** primarily for **government, defense, and intelligence agencies**.

---

## What Gotham Does

Gotham helps analysts **combine, analyze, and visualize massive amounts of data from different sources** in one system. Its goal is to turn scattered information into **actionable intelligence** for investigations, security operations, and military planning.

---

## Key Capabilities

- **Data Integration:** Connects many data sources such as databases, surveillance feeds, reports, and sensor data.
- **Intelligence Fusion:** Merges different datasets to reveal relationships between people, locations, events, and objects.
- **Link Analysis:** Visual graphs help analysts see networks and hidden connections.
- **Real-Time Analysis:** Supports rapid decision-making during security or military operations.
- **Operational Planning:** Helps commanders and analysts coordinate missions using data insights.

---

## Who Uses It

Gotham is typically used by:

- **Military organizations**
- **Intelligence agencies**
- **Law enforcement**
- **Counterterrorism units**
- **Border and national security agencies**

---

## Example Uses

- Tracking terrorist or criminal networks
- Analyzing battlefield intelligence
- Investigating financial crimes
- Coordinating emergency or defense operations

---

## Gotham vs. Foundry

Palantir has two main platforms:

| Platform | Designed For |
|---|---|
| **Palantir Gotham** | Government and defense agencies |
| **Palantir Foundry** | Commercial companies — banks, manufacturers, healthcare providers |

In simple terms: **Gotham helps security and intelligence agencies understand complex situations by connecting and analyzing huge datasets.**

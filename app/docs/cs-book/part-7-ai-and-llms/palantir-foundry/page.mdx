# Palantir Foundry

**Palantir Foundry** is a commercial data platform developed by **Palantir Technologies** that helps businesses **integrate, manage, and analyze large amounts of organizational data** in one unified system.

Unlike **Palantir Gotham**, which is designed mainly for defense and intelligence agencies, Foundry is built for **companies and commercial organizations**.

---

## What Foundry Does

Foundry allows organizations to bring together **data from many departments and systems** — such as finance, operations, supply chains, and customer data — and turn it into **real-time operational insights**.

Instead of data being scattered across different software tools, Foundry creates a **single operational data environment** where teams can analyze and act on information quickly.

---

## Key Capabilities

### 1. Data Integration

Foundry connects data from many sources, including:

- Databases
- ERP systems
- Cloud platforms
- Spreadsheets
- Operational systems

This eliminates **data silos** across an organization.

### 2. Data Modeling

The platform builds a **digital model of a company's operations**, allowing users to understand relationships between systems, processes, and outcomes.

### 3. Advanced Analytics & AI

Foundry integrates **machine learning and AI tools** that allow companies to:

- Forecast demand
- Detect risks
- Optimize production
- Automate decisions

### 4. Operational Applications

Companies can build **custom internal apps** on top of Foundry to support tasks such as:

- Supply chain planning
- Inventory management
- Financial analysis
- Manufacturing optimization

---

## Industries That Use Foundry

Businesses across multiple industries use Foundry, including:

- **Healthcare**
- **Energy**
- **Manufacturing**
- **Finance**
- **Aerospace**
- **Logistics**

For example, companies may use Foundry to **predict supply chain disruptions**, optimize factory production, or analyze financial performance.

---

## Gotham vs. Foundry

| Platform | Designed For |
|---|---|
| **Palantir Gotham** | Intelligence platform for military and security agencies |
| **Palantir Foundry** | Data operating system for businesses |

In simple terms: **Foundry helps companies turn scattered company data into decisions that improve operations and strategy.**

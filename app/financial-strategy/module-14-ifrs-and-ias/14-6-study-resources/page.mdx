# 14.6 — Recommended Study Resources

---

## Primary References

| Resource | Use |
|---|---|
| *Wiley IFRS 2024* by PKF International | Comprehensive practitioner reference — your bible |
| *Applying IFRS Standards* by Ruth Picker et al. (Wiley) | Conceptual understanding of the *why* behind each standard |
| ifrs.org | Free access to all standards — always verify against primary source |
| ACCA DipIFRS study materials | Structured learning with examination-quality questions |
| ICAP IFRS update seminars | Pakistan-specific application and regulatory context |

---

## For Financial Instruments Specifically

| Resource | Use |
|---|---|
| *IFRS 9: Financial Instruments* by EY (annual publication) | Deep technical treatment with worked examples |
| *PwC Manual of Accounting — Financial Instruments* | Practitioner-focused, voluminous, authoritative |
| BIS working papers on ECL implementation | Quantitative treatment of the ECL model |
| CFA Institute IFRS resources | Links IFRS 9 to investment analysis and portfolio management |

---

## Pakistan-Specific

| Resource | Use |
|---|---|
| SECP IFRS adoption circulars | Mandatory reading for any listed or regulated entity |
| ICAP Technical Releases | Local interpretations and implementation guidance |
| SBP BPRD circulars | Banking-specific IFRS modifications (relevant for relationships with banks) |

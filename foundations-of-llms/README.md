# Foundations of LLMs

A book on the foundations of large language models — from first principles through pretraining, fine-tuning, alignment, and deployment.

## Overview

This book builds an understanding of modern large language models (LLMs) from the ground up: what they are, how they're trained, how they're aligned to be useful and safe, and how they're deployed in production systems.
